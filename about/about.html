The Data
Hidden crisis
​Neurodivergent students face significant challenges in educational settings, often due to inadequate support systems. For instance, a study revealed that 36% of autistic undergraduate students who enrolled in 2019 did not complete their degree after three years, a rate higher than the 29% observed in the general student population. This elevated dropout rate underscores the pressing need for tailored support mechanisms to ensure the academic success and well-being of neurodivergent learners.
